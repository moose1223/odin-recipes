Vegatable Soup

Home

Ingredients

  • Large pot
  • 4 Cups chicken broth OR 4 Cups water and 4 tsp bouilon powder
  • 2 Carrots
  • 1 or 2 potatos
  • 1 stick of celery
  • Salt and pepper as needed
  1. Wash Vegtables
  2. Slice carrots and celery into bite sized chunks
  3. Peel and dice potatos into small chunks
  4. Add all ingredients to the pot, and let it boil on low for 1-2 hours. If the liquid gets too low, add more water
