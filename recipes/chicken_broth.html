Chicken Broth

Home

Ingridents

  • Large Pot with Lid
  • 8 Cups Water
  • Chicken Bones
  • Vegtable Scraps
  • 2 tsp salt
  • 1/2 tsp pepper
  • 1 tsp thyme

Instrutions

  1. Break Chicken Bones
  2. Add all ingridents to pot
  3. Set pot on high heat until boiling, then reduce heat, but make sure that it is still boiling
  4. Keep the pot boiling for at least 4 hours(longer is better), checking back occasiolnally and refilling water if it gets too low
  5. Once done, let the broth cool off a bit and then remove all solids
  6. You now have have broth to use for other recipies, such as Vegtable soup.
